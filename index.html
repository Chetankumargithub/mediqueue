Acuity score formula reference

Acuity Score (0–100) = O₂ Penalty (≤50) + HR Deviation (≤25) + Pain Level (≤15) + Age Risk (≤10) + Wait Boost (+15)
O₂ Saturation ≤ 50 pts
< 85% → 50 pts
85–89% → 40 pts
90–93% → 25 pts
94–95% → 10 pts
≥ 96% → 0 pts
Heart Rate ≤ 25 pts
>150 or <40 → 25 pts
>130 or <50 → 18 pts
>110 or <55 → 10 pts
>100 or <60 → 5 pts
Normal → 0 pts

9–10 → 15 pts
7–8 → 10 pts
5–6 → 5 pts
3–4 → 2 pts
1–2 → 0 pts

≥75 or ≤5 → 10 pts
≥65 or ≤12 → 6 pts
